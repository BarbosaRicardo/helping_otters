Helping Otters

Inspiring and empowering the next generation of developers and engineers

  • Learn More

Helping Otters is an after-school enrichment program focused on developing students' knowledge in the fields of mathematics, engineering, computer science, and software development through hands-on projects and one-on-one tutoring.

  • Process

Programs Offered




  • Mobile Applications

    Build your first mobile application in this 12 week program. Students will learn to build a Mobile application using Flutter, which will allow them to build mobile apps on Android and iOS at the same time.

    Meets Tuesdays & Thursdays at 3:30PM - 5:30PM

    Contact dan@helpingotters.com




  • Mathematics

    Schedule a one-on-one tutoring session to get an extra edge in any algebra, calculus, linear algebra, or differential equations course.

    Contact max@helpingotters.com or ricardo@helpingotters.com

    By Appointment Only




  • Robotics

    Spend 12 weeks building an automated robotic mouse with a team of 4 other students and participate in an end of the year competition where students race their robotic mice in a 16 x 16 maze.

    Meets Wednesdays at 3:30PM - 5:30PM

    Contact lindsey@helpingotters.com




  • Web Development

    Build a website for your class project, small business or just for fun! In this course, you will learn one of the following languages while you build your very own site.

    Languages: HTML, CSS, Javascript, React using Codesandbox

    This is an online class that meets on Zoom. Contact the instructor to schedule your appointment today

    Contact ricardo@helpingotters.com




  • Programming

    In this 6 month program, you will learn the fundamentals of coding, in the language of your choice.

    Languages: C#, C, Java, C++, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, AJAX, MySQL and Python

    Meets Mondays and Wednesdays at 3:30PM

    Contact max@helpingotters.com or lindsey@helpingotters.com




  • Language Tutoring

    Communication skills are vital in any profession, and using the wrong word can lead to an embarrassing moment. With this service students will receive additional help in Spanish.

    Meets Monday & Wednesday 3:30 PM - 5:30 PM

    Contact dan@helpingotters.com




  • PC Hardware

    Learn the fundamentals of PC Hardware and how it all works. Build a PC and learn to troubleshoot along the way in this 2 day course.

    Meets the first Weekend of every month at 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM

    Contact dan@helpingotters.com
